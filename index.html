Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide professional lighting gear with the basic rental?

Yes, our basic studio rental packages seamlessly include access to standard continuous lighting and strobe units. If you require advanced cinematic setups or precise lighting modifiers, please specify this during your booking so we can confidently prepare the floor.

Can we rent both floors of the studio simultaneously?

Absolutely. Many robust production teams choose to book both the natural light lifestyle floor and the upstairs acoustic video suites concurrently. This allows teams to efficiently capture multiple varying visual assets all in a single highly productive day.

Where exactly is the studio located?

Studio by Char is precisely situated in a remarkably quiet neighborhood in Chiang Mai, Thailand. This strategic location provides easy ground level loading access, very ample private parking, and beautifully avoids the constant noise pollution commonly found in the congested city center.
