Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide hair and makeup artists for productions?

Yes, we collaborate heavily with some of the absolute best professional hair and makeup artists natively based in Chiang Mai. We can seamlessly integrate their expert services into your complete overall production package, fully ensuring they perfectly match your creative vision.

Can we possibly shoot video and photography on the exact same day?

Absolutely. Many of our corporate clients actively maximize their precious time in the studio by effectively capturing both high-resolution stills and engaging video content simultaneously. Our experienced team is fully equipped to handle complex hybrid productions with zero delays.

How long does post-production normally take?

For standard product photography and professional headshot sessions, we typically deliver the final meticulously edited files within five to seven business days. Larger scale video productions or extensive fashion brand campaigns may require a more involved editing process taking roughly two to three calendar weeks.

Do you offer creative direction, or do I need to strictly bring my own ideas?

We highly encourage deep creative collaboration. If you possess a precise vision, we will simply execute it flawlessly. However, if you only have a rough conceptual idea, our dedicated team will actively develop a comprehensive mood board and storyboard to securely guide the entire production process from start to beautiful finish.
