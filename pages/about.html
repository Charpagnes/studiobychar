About the Studio

Studio by Char is a dedicated creative production studio deliberately built on the steadfast belief that truly exceptional visual work organically emerges from meticulously considered spaces. By combining professional high end equipment with a comfortable environment, we provide a haven for digital storytelling. We intimately understand exactly what visual creators strictly need because we are active creators ourselves. Our Creative Vision

Space Shapes Content

Studio by Char was purposely created to comprehensively fill a noticeable gap in the Chiang Mai creative landscape. Previously, locating a professional, elegant space where skilled photographers, videographers, and content creators could seamlessly work without making frustrating compromises was exceptionally difficult. We fundamentally believe that the physical environment powerfully shapes the resulting work, and that a well designed, intelligently managed studio effortlessly elevates the entire creative process.

Every single detail natively within our walls, from the precisely selected color of the archways to the exact quality of the modifiers, has been purposely chosen with maximum intention. We are sincerely not interested in being the largest warehouse studio in town. We are intensely interested in being the absolute best. We strive tirelessly to be the specific place where serious creators eagerly return again and again because they reliably know the resulting quality will perfectly match their highest expectations.

Founded by a Photographer

Studio by Char was founded by Charlotte, an experienced commercial photographer deeply passionate about beautiful visual design and technical excellence. After managing countless client productions in makeshift locations, she realized the pressing local need for a dedicated, beautiful space equipped specifically for modern production demands.

Charlotte leads the resident commercial production team, ensuring that every brand campaign entering the building benefits from her rigorous attention to detail and creative styling expertise. The studio is a direct reflection of her personal commitment to elevating the visual standards available within Chiang Mai.

Common Inquiries

Are beginners totally welcome to successfully rent and comfortably use the studio?

Yes, absolutely. We welcome creators spanning absolutely all experience levels warmly. If you have genuinely never utilized professional studio lighting before naturally, our remarkably friendly team will happily provide a complimentary basic tutorial comprehensively covering how to effectively operate the included lighting modifiers and backgrounds during your initial walk through process.

Can I confidently arrange to personally tour the space prior to committing to a booking?

Yes. We actively encourage prospective commercial clients to properly visit and tour the physical space whenever they can. Please kindly message us using WhatsApp or email us directly to accurately set up a convenient time to view both our lifestyle setups and specialized podcast environments.

Where exactly in Chiang Mai is the studio physically located?

We purposefully located our studio slightly offset from the heavily congested city center to ensure quiet surroundings conducive perfectly for clear audio recording and to provide ample private parking perfectly suited for safely unloading heavy camera gear. You can quickly refer extensively to the map natively embedded in our website footer for precise navigational directions.
