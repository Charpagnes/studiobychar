Frequently Asked Questions

Does the studio rental include access to professional lighting?

Yes, our downstairs studio rental rigorously includes a solid base level of professional continuous lighting and strobe equipment. Essential light modifiers and stands are openly available directly on the shooting floor for you to use instantly.

Can we drastically adjust the natural sunlight entering the set throughout the day?

We thoughtfully equipped the main lifestyle floor with entirely modular diffusion curtains and heavy block out blinds. This intelligently allows you to effectively manipulate the streaming afternoon natural light, or selectively block it absolutely to execute completely controlled strobe setups.

Is the downstairs lifestyle studio acoustically soundproofed?

The downstairs floor heavily favors striking visual aesthetics and natural lifestyle lighting. For precisely critical audio recordings, we strongly recommend renting the acoustically treated podcast suites seamlessly located on the second floor above.
